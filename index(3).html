About
Uses multiple free databases for maximum product coverage:
• Open Food Facts (3M+ food products) — primary source
• Open Products Facts — general consumer goods
• Open Beauty Facts — cosmetics & personal care
• Open Pet Food Facts — pet food
• UPC Item DB — broad global product catalog
• FDA GRAS Database — additive safety cross-check (on-device)
• Google Search — last-resort link (no API)
Ratings are computed on-device from nutrition facts + ingredients.
Tip: This app works best as an installed PWA (Add to Home Screen).
